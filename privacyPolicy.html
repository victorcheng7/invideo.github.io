What personal information we collect

Absolutely nothing.

When you use this extension to search within Youtube we send your request directly to Google’s servers and provide you with the results.

We don’t store or transfer any of your personal information remotely or locally on your own device as it may lack the proper encryption required to store sensitive information.

Read more about Youtube Privacy Policy.

Any changes to this Policy will be solely for improving your experience when using the extension. In addition, future changes will be updated on this page which we made accessible from the extension menu and the extension page on the Chrome Web Store for your convenience.
